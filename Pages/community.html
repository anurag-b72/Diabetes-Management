We Dia-buddy

We love to be sweet

We have created a website dedicated to the diabetics. Spreading awareness.With diabetes, your body doesn't make enough insulin or can't use it as well as it should. When there isn't enough insulin or cells stop responding to insulin, too much blood sugar stays in your bloodstream. Over time, that can cause serious health problems, such as heart disease, vision loss, and kidney disease.


Symptoms


Sudden loss of weight

Fruitish smell from mouth

Weakness and blurry vision

What next


Contact your doctor asap

Don't hesitate to take medicine or insulin

Drink enough water

Living


Yoga and meditation

Positive attitude

Regular check-ups
